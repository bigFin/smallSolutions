# Portfolio Priorities

This is a working editorial ranking for the site. The goal is not completeness. The goal is to surface the projects that best support the site's current purpose:

- show strong technical judgment
- show translational R&D ability
- show range across embedded systems, analytics, biotech, and operations
- show work that became operationally or economically useful

## Tier 1: Core Proof

These are the projects that best establish the site's value proposition and should carry most of the narrative weight.

### 1. Avenue Intelligence

Why it matters:
- strongest current work
- combines embedded systems, analytics, ML, infrastructure, and operations
- shows judgment under real constraints
- most clearly speaks to current trajectory and future-facing capability

Role on site:
- flagship project
- likely deserves first position on homepage and `/work`

### 2. Plantlet Finishing Chambers

Why it matters:
- strong translational biotech / controls example
- shows experimental work becoming a real production input
- contrasts well with Avenue without weakening the through-line

Role on site:
- second featured project
- supports the broader "research to operations" theme

### 3. Meristem to Production

Why it matters:
